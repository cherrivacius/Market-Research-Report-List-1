<p><ul><li>Latex Tourniquet</li><li>TPE Tourniquet</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The manual tourniquet market primarily consists of two types: latex and TPE (thermoplastic elastomer) tourniquets. Latex tourniquets are commonly used due to their elasticity and strength, providing reliable occlusion during medical procedures. However, they may cause allergic reactions in some patients. TPE tourniquets, on the other hand, are hypoallergenic, durable, and more environmentally friendly, making them increasingly popular. Both types serve essential roles in controlling bleeding and ensuring patient safety in clinical settings.</p></p>
