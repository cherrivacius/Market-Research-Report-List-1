<p><ul><li>Chemical Testing</li><li>Physical Testing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The pharmaceutical release testing market comprises two main types: chemical testing and physical testing. Chemical testing involves analyzing the composition, potency, and purity of pharmaceutical products to ensure they meet specified standards and regulatory requirements. This can include assays for active ingredients and impurities. Physical testing focuses on evaluating the physical characteristics of drugs, such as dissolution rates, stability, and texture. Together, these testing types ensure the safety, efficacy, and quality of pharmaceutical products before they reach consumers.</p></p>
