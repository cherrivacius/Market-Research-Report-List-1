<p><p>The Organic Package Substrates market encompasses various types, including MCP/UTCSP (Multi-Chip Package/Ultra Thin Chip Scale Package), which facilitate compact designs for high-performance applications. FC-CSP (Flip Chip Chip Scale Package) offers a smaller footprint and enhanced thermal performance. SiP (System in Package) integrates multiple components in a single package, improving efficiency. PBGA/CSP (Plastic Ball Grid Array/Chip Scale Package) ensures effective connectivity. BOC (Bare Die on Chip) allows for direct die attachment, and FMC (Flexible Multi-layer Circuit) provides flexibility. Other variants cater to specialized needs in electronics packaging.</p></p>
