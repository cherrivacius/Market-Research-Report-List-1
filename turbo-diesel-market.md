<p><p>The Turbo Diesel market primarily features two types: Mono Turbo and Twin Turbo systems. Mono Turbo systems use a single turbocharger to boost engine performance and efficiency, making them common in standard diesel engines. In contrast, Twin Turbo systems employ two turbochargers, typically enhancing power delivery and reducing turbo lag, resulting in improved acceleration and responsiveness. These systems cater to different segments, with Mono Turbo being more prevalent in everyday vehicles and Twin Turbo often found in performance-oriented models.</p></p>
