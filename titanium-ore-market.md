<p><p>The titanium ore market comprises three primary types: ilmenite, titanium slag, and rutile. Ilmenite is a key source, primarily used for producing titanium dioxide, while titanium slag, produced by smelting ilmenite, has a higher titanium content and is valuable for metal production. Rutile, the most titanium-rich ore, is sought after for its superior quality and is used in various applications, including pigments and metal alloys. Each type plays a crucial role in meeting industrial demand for titanium-related products.</p></p>
