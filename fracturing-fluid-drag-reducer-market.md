<p><p>The fracturing fluid drag reducer market consists of two main types: granular and emulsion-based drag reducers. Granular fracturing fluid drag reducers, typically solid additives, enhance fluid flow properties by reducing friction in the wellbore, enabling more efficient resource extraction. Emulsion type fracturing fluid drag reducers are liquid-based additives that improve fluid stability and reduce viscosity, promoting better mixing and transport of proppants during hydraulic fracturing. Both types are essential in optimizing oil and gas extraction processes.</p></p>
