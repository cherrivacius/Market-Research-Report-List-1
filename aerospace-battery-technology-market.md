<p><p>The aerospace battery technology market includes three main types: Nickel-Cadmium (NiCd), Lead Acid, and Lithium-Ion batteries. NiCd batteries are known for their robustness and ability to perform in extreme temperatures, but they often face regulatory scrutiny due to cadmium toxicity. Lead Acid batteries are cost-effective and reliable but have heavier weight and shorter lifespans. Lithium-Ion batteries dominate the market due to their high energy density, lightweight, and longer cycle life, making them increasingly preferred for modern aerospace applications.</p></p>
