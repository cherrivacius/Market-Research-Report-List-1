<p><ul><li>One-channel ABS</li><li>Two-channel ABS</li><li>Three-channel ABS</li><li>Four-channel ABS</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Antilock Braking System (ABS) control modules are categorized based on their channel configuration: </p><p>1. One-channel ABS controls a single wheel, mainly found in older vehicles.</p><p>2. Two-channel ABS manages front wheels collectively and rear wheels collectively, suitable for light trucks.</p><p>3. Three-channel ABS operates independently on two front wheels while controlling rear wheels together, enhancing stability.</p><p>4. Four-channel ABS offers individual control for all wheels, providing optimal braking performance and safety in modern vehicles, minimizing skid risks during emergency braking.</p></p>
