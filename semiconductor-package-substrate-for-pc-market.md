<p><ul><li>FC-BGA</li><li>FC-CSP</li><li>WB BGA</li><li>WB CSP</li></ul></p>
<p><p>In the PC market, semiconductor package substrates are vital for connecting integrated circuits to the motherboard. Key types include FC-BGA (Flip-Chip Ball Grid Array), which offers high-performance and compact designs; FC-CSP (Flip-Chip Chip Scale Package), known for its small size and integration; WB BGA (Wafer Ball Grid Array), which allows for thinner profiles and efficient heat dissipation; and WB CSP (Wafer Chip Scale Package), providing minimal footprint and enhanced connectivity. These technologies cater to varying performance and space requirements in computing devices.</p></p>
