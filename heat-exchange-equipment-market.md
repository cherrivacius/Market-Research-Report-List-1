<p><p>The heat-exchange equipment market comprises various types tailored for different applications. Shell and tube heat exchangers feature a tubular design for efficient heat transfer between fluids, making them ideal for high-pressure conditions. Plate heat exchangers utilize thin plates to maximize surface area, offering high efficiency in compact spaces, suitable for food processing and HVAC applications. Air-cooled heat exchangers use ambient air to cool fluids, often found in power plants and refineries, promoting energy efficiency without water consumption.</p></p>
