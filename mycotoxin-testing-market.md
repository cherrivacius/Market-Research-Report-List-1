<p><strong>The Mycotoxin Testing Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>HPLC-Based Technology</li><li>LC-MS/MS-Based Technology</li><li>Immunoassay-Based Technology</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The mycotoxin testing market consists of several key technologies. HPLC-based technology utilizes high-performance liquid chromatography to separate and quantify mycotoxins in food and feed samples. LC-MS/MS-based technology combines liquid chromatography with tandem mass spectrometry for highly sensitive and specific detection of multiple mycotoxins simultaneously. Immunoassay-based technology relies on antibody-antigen reactions to identify and quantify mycotoxins, offering rapid results. Each method caters to different testing needs and regulatory requirements, driving advancements in food safety and quality control.</p></p>
