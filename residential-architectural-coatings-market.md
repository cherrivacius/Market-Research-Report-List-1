<p><p>The Residential Architectural Coatings market encompasses various types of products used for protective and aesthetic purposes in homes. Water-soluble coatings are easier to clean up and emit fewer volatile organic compounds (VOCs). Solvent coatings offer durability and resistance but may have higher VOC emissions. Emulsion coatings combine water and oils, providing good adhesion and flexibility, making them ideal for different surfaces. Each type serves specific needs in residential applications, catering to environmental regulations and consumer preferences.</p></p>
