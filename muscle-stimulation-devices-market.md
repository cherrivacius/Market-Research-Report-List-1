<p><p>Muscle stimulation devices encompass various types, including Neuromuscular Electric Stimulators (NMES) and Transcutaneous Electric Nerve Stimulators (TENS). NMES targets muscle activation for rehabilitation and strength building, using electrical impulses to stimulate nerves and improve muscle function. TENS focuses on pain management by delivering low-voltage electrical currents to interrupt pain signals. Other devices may include stimulation units for specific therapies, such as muscle recovery or enhancing physical performance, catering to diverse user needs in both medical and personal settings.</p></p>
