<p><p>The automatic candy packaging machine market comprises two primary types: vertical and horizontal machines. Vertical type machines operate by feeding the candy from the top down, utilizing gravity to enhance efficiency and reduce space, making them ideal for lightweight products. In contrast, horizontal type machines use a side-loading mechanism that allows for faster packaging of larger or heavier candies. Both types cater to diverse production needs, ensuring optimal packaging solutions tailored to varying candy shapes and sizes.</p></p>
