<p><p>Classroom Management Systems (CMS) can be categorized into two main market types: on-premise and cloud-based. On-premise systems are installed and operated locally on the school’s servers, providing full control over data and customization, but require significant IT resources. Cloud-based systems, on the other hand, are hosted online, allowing easier access, scalability, and automatic updates. They typically reduce the need for robust infrastructure while facilitating collaboration and remote management, making them increasingly popular among educational institutions seeking flexibility and cost-effectiveness.</p></p>
