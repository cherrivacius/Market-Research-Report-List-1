<p><strong>The 3D Optical Microscopes Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>White Light Interferometry (WLI)</li><li>Laser Scanning Confocal Microscopy (LSCM)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The 3D optical microscopes market includes techniques like White Light Interferometry (WLI) and Laser Scanning Confocal Microscopy (LSCM). WLI utilizes broad-spectrum light to achieve high-resolution surface profilometry, ideal for measuring topography and roughness in various materials. In contrast, LSCM employs focused laser beams to capture detailed 3D images by scanning the specimen point by point, providing enhanced contrast and depth resolution. Both methods are essential in applications ranging from materials science to biological research.</p></p>
