<p><p>The Electronic Fuel Injection (EFI) systems market is primarily categorized into two types: Single-point Injection Systems and Multi-point Injection Systems. Single-point injection delivers fuel to a single point before entering the intake manifold, providing a simpler design and reduced cost. Conversely, Multi-point injection supplies fuel directly to each individual cylinder, enhancing precision and performance. This refined fuel delivery in Multi-point systems improves combustion efficiency and reduces emissions, catering to the growing demand for eco-friendly vehicles and advanced automotive technology.</p></p>
