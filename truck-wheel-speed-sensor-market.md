<p><strong>The Truck Wheel Speed Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Hall Type</li><li>Magnetic Electric Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The truck wheel speed sensor market includes two main types: Hall effect sensors and magnetic electric sensors. Hall effect sensors utilize a magnetic field to produce an output signal, providing accurate and consistent speed readings. In contrast, magnetic electric sensors rely on changing magnetic fields generated by rotating components to measure speed, often being less sensitive to external factors. Both types are essential for improving vehicle stability, enhancing anti-lock braking systems, and optimizing overall truck performance.</p></p>
