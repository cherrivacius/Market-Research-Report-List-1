<p><strong>The Food Grade Silicone Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>"Hot Vulcanized Silicone (HTV)"</li><li>"Room Temperature Vulcanized Silicone (RTV)"</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The food-grade silicone market is primarily divided into two types: Hot Vulcanized Silicone (HTV) and Room Temperature Vulcanized Silicone (RTV). HTV is processed using heat and pressure, offering excellent durability and high-temperature resistance, making it ideal for applications like baking mats and molds. RTV, on the other hand, cures at room temperature, providing flexibility and ease of use, suitable for sealants, gaskets, and kitchen utensils. Both types ensure safety and compliance for food contact applications, catering to diverse consumer needs.</p></p>
