<p><p>The fire suppressant market encompasses various types designed for specific fire scenarios. Foam fire suppressants are effective for flammable liquid fires, creating a barrier between fuel and oxygen. Powder fire suppressants, often dry chemical agents, smother flames effectively across diverse combustible materials. Carbon dioxide fire suppressants displace oxygen to suffocate fires, ideal for electrical and flammable liquid fires. Wet chemical fire suppressants, primarily for kitchen fires, cool and saponify oils. Other agents include water mist and aerosol suppressants for specialized applications.</p></p>
