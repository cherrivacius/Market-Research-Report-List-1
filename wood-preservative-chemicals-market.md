<p><strong>The Wood Preservative Chemicals Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Water-Based Preservative</li><li>Oil-Based Preservative</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The wood preservative chemicals market is primarily categorized into water-based and oil-based preservatives. Water-based preservatives are eco-friendly, quick-drying, and suitable for interior applications, offering good penetration and reduced toxicity. They typically contain lower levels of volatile organic compounds (VOCs). In contrast, oil-based preservatives provide enhanced durability and weather resistance, making them ideal for exterior applications. They penetrate deeper into the wood, offering long-lasting protection against decay and pests but generally have higher VOC content.</p></p>
