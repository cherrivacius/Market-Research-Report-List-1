The market comprises three main types: </p><p>1. **Reflux Capsule**: This small, swallowable device measures reflux events over an extended period, providing data on esophageal acid exposure.</p><p>2. **Reflux Probe**: A traditional method where a probe is placed in the esophagus to continuously monitor acid levels.</p><p>3. **Reflux Catheter**: This flexible tube is inserted through the nose or mouth for real-time monitoring, enabling detailed analysis of reflux episodes.</p></p>
