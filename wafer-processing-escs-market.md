<p><strong>The Wafer Processing ESCs Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Coulomb Type</li><li>Johnsen-Rahbek (JR) Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>In the wafer processing equipment market, two notable types of Electrostatic Chuck (ESC) technologies are the Coulomb Type and Johnsen-Rahbek (JR) Type. The Coulomb Type ESC utilizes electrostatic forces for wafer gripping, ensuring precise alignment and stability during processing, which enhances efficiency. In contrast, the JR Type employs a combination of electrostatic and mechanical forces, providing heightened thermal stability and customization for various wafer sizes and materials. Both types play crucial roles in semiconductor manufacturing, influencing productivity and yield.</p></p>
