<p><p>The thermostable polymerases market comprises various enzyme types, each serving distinct functions in molecular biology. Taq polymerase, derived from Thermus aquaticus, is widely used for PCR due to its heat stability and efficiency. Bst polymerases, originating from Bacillus stearothermophilus, are favored for isothermal amplification methods. Pfu polymerases, from Pyrococcus furiosus, offer high fidelity and proofreading ability, making them ideal for applications requiring accuracy. Together, these polymerases cater to diverse research and diagnostic needs in genomics and biotechnology.</p></p>
