<p><p>The Remote Noise Monitoring System market is segmented into wired and wireless types. Wired systems utilize physical cables to connect sensors to a central monitoring unit, offering reliability and minimal interference. However, their installation can be cumbersome and limited by distance. In contrast, wireless systems use radio frequencies or other technologies to transmit data, allowing for flexible installations and easier scalability. This versatility makes wireless solutions increasingly popular in various environments, providing real-time noise data without extensive infrastructure.</p></p>
