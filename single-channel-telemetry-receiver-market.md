<p><p>The Single Channel Telemetry Receiver market is segmented into two main types: wireless and wired telemetry receivers. Wireless telemetry receivers utilize radio frequency or infrared signals to transmit data without the need for physical connections, offering flexibility and ease of installation. In contrast, wired telemetry receivers rely on direct cable connections for data transmission, providing reliable and stable communication, particularly in environments with high interference. Both types serve various applications, including industrial monitoring, healthcare, and environmental data collection.</p></p>
