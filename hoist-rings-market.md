<p><strong>The Hoist Rings Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Center-pull</li><li>Side-pull</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Hoist rings are categorized into two main market types: center-pull and side-pull. Center-pull hoist rings are designed for lifting objects directly from the center, providing a balanced load distribution and stability. They are ideal for vertical lifts. In contrast, side-pull hoist rings allow for lifting from the side, giving operators more flexibility in maneuvering loads and accommodating specific lifting angles. Both types serve distinct applications in industrial settings, improving efficiency and safety in material handling.</p></p>
