<p><p>The standard probe card market consists of three main types: vertical, MEMS, and others. Vertical probe cards utilize a straight vertical alignment that enhances density and is ideal for high-volume production. MEMS probe cards leverage micro-electromechanical systems technology for improved precision and flexibility, often used in advanced semiconductor testing. The "other" category encompasses various specialized probe cards tailored to specific applications, including high-frequency and custom designs, catering to niche markets and unique testing requirements in the semiconductor industry.</p></p>
