<p><ul><li>PE Synthetic Turf</li><li>PP Synthetic Turf</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The eco-friendly turf market mainly comprises two types: PE (Polyethylene) synthetic turf and PP (Polypropylene) synthetic turf. PE synthetic turf is known for its durability and realistic appearance, making it ideal for residential lawns and sports fields. In contrast, PP synthetic turf is lighter and more affordable, suitable for low-traffic areas and decorative purposes. Both types offer reduced environmental impact, as they are recyclable and require less water and maintenance compared to natural grass, promoting sustainability in landscaping.</p></p>
