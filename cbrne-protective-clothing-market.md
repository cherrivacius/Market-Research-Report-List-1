<p><p>CBRNe protective clothing is classified into two primary types: gas-tight and gas-permeable. Gas-tight clothing provides a complete seal against hazardous agents, ensuring no gases or vapors can penetrate, making it ideal for high-risk environments. On the other hand, gas-permeable clothing allows for some breathability while still offering protection from chemical or biological threats, making it suitable for prolonged wear in less intense situations. Both types play a crucial role in ensuring safety during CBRNe incidents.</p></p>
