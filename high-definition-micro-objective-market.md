<p><p>High Definition Micro Objectives are specialized lenses used in microscopy, categorized by their power levels. Low Power Objectives typically offer magnification up to 4x or 10x, ideal for viewing larger specimens. Medium Power Objectives, ranging from 20x to 40x, provide greater detail while still allowing for a broader field of view. High Power Objectives, usually 100x or more, are designed for observing fine details in small samples, enabling precise examination in fields like biology and materials science.</p></p>
