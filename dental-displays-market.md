<p><p>The dental displays market primarily comprises two types: LCD (Liquid Crystal Display) and LED (Light Emitting Diode) technologies. LCD displays utilize liquid crystals to produce images and offer excellent color accuracy and resolution, making them suitable for detailed dental imagery. LED displays, on the other hand, enhance brightness and energy efficiency, providing vibrant colors and better contrast, ideal for presentations and patient education. Both types are increasingly adopted in dental practices for diagnostic and marketing purposes.</p></p>
