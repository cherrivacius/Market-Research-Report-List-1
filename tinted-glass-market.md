<p><ul><li>Common Glass</li><li>Toughened Glass</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The tinted glass market is divided into two main types: common glass and toughened glass. Common tinted glass features shades that reduce glare and enhance aesthetics but lacks structural strength. In contrast, toughened tinted glass is processed to withstand high stress and impact, making it suitable for safety applications. Both types offer UV protection and energy efficiency, yet toughened glass is preferred for architectural and automotive uses due to its durability, while common glass is often utilized in decorative applications.</p></p>
