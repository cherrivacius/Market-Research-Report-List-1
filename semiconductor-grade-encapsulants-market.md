<p><p>The semiconductor grade encapsulants market is primarily categorized into one-component and two-component formulations. One-component encapsulants are pre-mixed and ready for use, providing ease of application and consistent properties. They typically cure through a heat or moisture process. In contrast, two-component encapsulants require mixing two separate components before application, offering greater customization in properties like viscosity and curing time. This flexibility makes them suitable for specific applications, ensuring enhanced performance and reliability in semiconductor packaging.</p></p>
